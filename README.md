# SDK Local 🇿🇦

**Connecting Local Businesses with the Heart of the Community.**

SDK Local is a digital platform designed to bridge the gap between service providers and residents in South Africa. Our mission is to empower local entrepreneurs—from Phuthaditjhaba to Johannesburg—by making their services discoverable, accessible, and professional.

---

## 🚀 Our Mission
To stimulate the local economy by providing a centralized hub where community members can find verified service providers and local businesses can grow their digital footprint.

## 🛠 Project Features (MVP)
* **Business Directory:** A searchable database of local services.
* **Service Verification:** Ensuring trust between providers and customers.
* **Community Integration:** Focused tools for South African neighborhoods.
* **Future Modules:** Expansion into entertainment and gaming utility (Golden Dragon Casino & Lottery).

## 📅 Roadmap
* **Phase 1:** Initial Setup & Repository Structure (Current)
* **Phase 2:** Developer Environment & UI Mockups
* **Phase 3:** Official Launch - **April 18, 2026**
